If you're traveling to OwO City, you might want to know some places to go.

Here are some of the best!


1. OwO Trade Center (7 Main St.)



Currently, the OwO Trade Center is the tallest building in OwO City, with 15 floors, standing 67 meters (219 feet) tall.



2. Owozon Tower (1 Main St.)



(view from the roof)


Owozon Tower was the first building ever built in OwO City, and was the tallest for nearly two weeks. It has 9 floors and stands at 40 meters. (131 feet)
